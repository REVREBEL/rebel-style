Unveiling the Importance of Distribution Strategy for Independent Hotels

In this CTRLShift article, we discuss the significance of distribution strategy for independent hotels and the critical role regular audits play in maximizing its effectiveness. By offering an in-depth look into your strategy, audits can reveal hidden revenue opportunities and potential issues before they become significant obstacles.
